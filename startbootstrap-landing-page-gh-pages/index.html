Circuit Validation/Design

 

Circuit Validation/Design

Validation is often neglected in the grand scheme of things as it isn't directly seen most of the time in the eyes of the consumer. However, this is thinking could not be more wrong. Good validation ensures that a product release is free of bugs and works exactly as expected when released to the public.

Circuit design and validation go hand and hand. Good circuit design combined with good validation helps ensure a solid product and reduces the chance of angry customers when the product is released.
